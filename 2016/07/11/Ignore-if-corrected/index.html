Ignore if corrected ...

Jul 11, 2016

I was taking a sip in the balcony of my first floor residential quarter in the evening. This is the complex of four blocks of 8 quarters surrounding one square lawn. In the lawn benches are placed for lounging. The moment I witnessed one of my fellow instructor talking on phone with his daughter who is 2nd grader. He had the opportunity to join as trainee instructor in the institution meant for the clerks. At the mean time one of my colleagues joined me jovially entering the room. We just has our gossips that we concentrated on the fluctuating discourse of the whitish strong built seems to be educated fellow. We kept mum and listened to the explanation and direction being given to his daughter.

His daughter was assigned home work to write one paragraph on the topic ‘Drought’. He mentioned with full authenticity that where there is heavy rain and flood like condition is known as draught. Actually he was explaining the difficult words, perhaps making her write that this word came in follow through. Our notice was all about that he is a good father that besides being away for the time being he is providing every help and assistance to he daughter in her school activities.

We were shun after listening his argument but did not interfere him of being scared. However the gesture that embodies it looked as if he was wrong somewhere. The next day I and my friend were amused to see his in front of the class room meant to teach grammar. It stopped only when we indicated him to understand the correct meaning of ‘Drought’.

The education is life long process. It continues from cradle to pyre. Today formal and informal education features in almost all the societies. The world of knowledge has expended. There have been numerous sources of acquiring knowledge and is easily accessible too. The minds today is well equipped with sophisticated fields.

To shy and hide the ignorance may lead to the dark arena of knowledge. The act father did will not simply be forgotten as the child in her environment will definitely come to know by leaving no space to sorry. Chances are you being hated against teachers trust. Also the conflict in mind will abstract the space of right knowledge and may have no chance to take help of digital dictionary.

The society which allowed us to come across such situation where we could not directly interfere in his on line tuition there needs more openness and close contacts for the persons in the world of knowledge.
